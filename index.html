Mixed Martial Arts

About MMA

Mixed martial arts (MMA) is a full-contact combat sport that allows striking and grappling, both standing and on the ground, using techiques from other combat sports and martial arts.
The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) is the largest MMA promotion in the world and features the top-ranked fighters of the sport.
